To get admission into these colleges, students need to pass Class 12th with a good percentage and qualify in a sports entrance exam. Some of the common sports entrance exams in India include the All India Entrance Examination for Sports (AIEES), the Netaji Subhas National Institute of Sports Entrance Exam (NSNIS EE), and the Lakshmibai National Institute of Physical Education Entrance Exam (LNIPE EE).

EXAMS FOR THE COLLEGES:

Here are some of the exams that sports students in India can take:
• All India Entrance Examination for Sports (AIEES): AIEES is a national-level sports entrance exam conducted by the Sports Authority of India (SAI) for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate programs in sports and physical education at various SAI and non-SAI institutions in India.
• Netaji Subhas National Institute of Sports Entrance Exam (NSNIS EE): NSNIS EE is a national-level sports entrance exam conducted by the Netaji Subhas National Institute of Sports (NSNIS), Patiala for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate programs in sports and physical education at NSNIS.
• Lakshmibai National Institute of Physical Education Entrance Exam (LNIPE EE): LNIPE EE is a national-level sports entrance exam conducted by the Lakshmibai National Institute of Physical Education (LNIPE), Gwalior for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate programs in sports and physical education at LNIPE.
• State-level sports entrance exams: Many states in India also conduct their own state-level sports entrance exams for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate programs in sports and physical education at state-run institutions.
• University-level sports entrance exams: Some universities in India also conduct their own university-level sports entrance exams for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate programs in sports and physical education.

SUBJECTS FOR THE LAW STUDENTS:

Sports students need to study a variety of subjects, including:
• Core sports science subjects: These subjects provide the foundation for understanding how the human body works and how to improve athletic performance. Core sports science subjects include anatomy, physiology, biomechanics, and nutrition.
• Specialized sports subjects: These subjects teach students about the specific skills and techniques required for their chosen sport. For example, a basketball player might study shooting, dribbling, and passing, while a soccer player might study ball control, passing, and shooting.
• Sports coaching and management subjects: These subjects teach students how to coach and manage athletes. These subjects are important for students who are interested in pursuing a career in sports coaching or management.

INTERNSHIP-APPORTUNITY:

There are many internship opportunities for sports students in India. Here are some of the most common places where sports students can intern:
• Sports academies and training centers: Sports academies and training centers offer internships to sports students in a variety of areas, such as coaching, sports science, and sports management.
• Sports federations: Sports federations offer internships to sports students in a variety of areas, such as event management, marketing, and communications.
• Sports media organizations: Sports media organizations offer internships to sports students in a variety of areas, such as journalism, photography, and videography.
• Government sports bodies: Government sports bodies, such as the Sports Authority of India (SAI), offer internships to sports students in a variety of areas, such as sports administration, sports development, and sports research.
• Sports companies: Sports companies offer internships to sports students in a variety of areas, such as marketing, sales, and product development.
To find internship opportunities, sports students can contact their sports coach, sports federation, or university's career services office. They can also search for internships online or network with sports professionals.
